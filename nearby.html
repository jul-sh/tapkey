Nearby passkey flow

Open this page from the tapkey app to run a passkey ceremony for a key you want to recover on this Mac.

This page is stateless. After it loads, it does not send the PRF result to a server.

What happens

If the passkey is not on this Mac, choose iPhone or nearby device when WebKit offers it.

  1. Tap continue.
  2. Approve the passkey prompt on this Mac or on your iPhone.
  3. tapkey receives the result and derives the key locally.
The QR flow asks your iPhone to approve the passkey ceremony. Your passkey stays on the device that already has it.
